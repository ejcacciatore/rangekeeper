## FEATURES SECTION

### The KeyLedger Platform

#### 🔐 SoftLock — Buyer Verification Layer

**The problem:**  
Buyers say "I'm serious" but agents don't know if they can close. Proof of funds letters are static, stale, easy to fake. Offers get submitted without real credibility signals.

**The KeyLedger solution:**  
Mobile verification flow. Buyer scans property QR code → verifies funds privately via Plaid → deposits small binder via mobile escrow → verified badge strengthens offer. Agent sees "Verified Buyer" status without accessing raw bank data.

**What agents get:**
- ✅ Verified buyer list (live fund status, no stale POF letters)
- ✅ Credibility signals for listing agents and sellers
- ✅ Faster closings (verified = less due diligence friction)

**What buyers get:**
- ✅ Stand out in competitive markets
- ✅ Private verification (agent never sees account numbers)
- ✅ One verification, multiple offers (reusable across properties)

#### 📝 HomeLedger — Immutable Transaction Timeline

**Your deals become structured data, not scattered chaos.**

Every transaction captures:
- **Event log:** Listing date, showing appointments, offer submitted, inspection completed, mortgage approved, closing date
- **Documents:** Contracts, addendums, disclosures, reports, final HUD — all timestamped and attached to specific events
- **Participants:** Buyer, seller, buyer's agent, listing agent, lender, attorney, inspector, appraiser
- **Outcomes:** Sale price, days on market, contingencies waived, inspection findings

**Why this matters:**
- 🔍 Post-closing intelligence: "How long do deals typically take with this lender?"
- 📈 Performance insights: "Which inspectors delay closings?"
- 🤝 Relationship graph: Build trusted network of high-performing providers
- 🚀 Future automation: Predictive timelines, smart provider recommendations, market intelligence

**HomeLedger isn't just workflow capture — it's your proprietary transaction dataset.**

## HOW IT WORKS

### For Agents

**1. Create Account**  
Sign up in 2 minutes. Connect your brokerage info. Invite your team (optional).

**2. Add a Transaction**  
New listing? New buyer client? Start a deal timeline. Add property, participants, key dates.

**3. Invite Your Client**  
Buyer gets mobile link → verifies funds via SoftLock (optional) → accesses client portal for updates.

**4. Coordinate the Deal**  
Upload documents. Log events (offer, inspection, appraisal). Track contingencies. Send updates.

**5. Close & Capture**  
Deal closes. Timeline is complete. You now have structured intelligence for future deals.

**Repeat.** Every transaction makes you smarter.

---

### For Buyers

**1. Get Invited by Your Agent**  
Agent sends you a KeyLedger link for the property you're pursuing.

**2. Verify Your Funds (Optional)**  
Scan QR code → connect bank via Plaid → verify funds privately → deposit small binder ($500-2,000) → get "Verified Buyer" badge.

**3. Submit Offer**  
Your offer includes KeyLedger verification badge. Listing agent and seller see you're credible.

**4. Track Your Deal**  
Access your client portal. See timeline updates. Upload requested docs. Message your agent.

**5. Close Confidently**  
No surprises. Full visibility. Structured process.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to migrate all my old transactions?**  
A: No. Start fresh. KeyLedger is forward-looking. Add new deals as they come. After 6 months, you'll have rich intelligence.

**Q: Does this replace my brokerage's transaction management system?**  
A: No. KeyLedger complements your existing tools. Think of it as the coordination + intelligence layer on top.

**Q: What if my buyer doesn't want to verify funds?**  
A: SoftLock is optional. They can still use KeyLedger's client portal to track the deal. Verification just adds a competitive edge.

**Q: How does SoftLock work without exposing bank account details?**  
A: We use Plaid's verification API. Your agent sees "Verified: $X available" — not account numbers, not transaction history.

**Q: Is this RESPA compliant?**  
A: Yes. KeyLedger does not provide settlement services, only coordination and verification. Marketplace referral fees follow RESPA guidelines.

**Q: Can I white-label this for my brokerage?**  
A: Yes, on the Brokerage plan. Custom branding, your logo, your domain.

**Q: Do you integrate with Dotloop, DocuSign, or SkySlope?**  
A: Coming soon. For now, you can upload signed docs from those platforms into KeyLedger's timeline.

**Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?**  
A: You can export all transaction timelines and documents. We retain data for 90 days post-cancellation, then delete per your request.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**  
A: Phase 2. For now, KeyLedger is a mobile-responsive web app (works great on phones).
