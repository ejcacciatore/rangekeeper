# 📱 RangeKeeper — Your Personal Assignment Helper

**Made for:** Massimo (UA Student)  
**Made by:** Dad  
**What it does:** Helps you never miss an assignment or deadline

---

## 🎯 What This Does For You

RangeKeeper watches your Blackboard and reminds you when assignments are coming up.

**You get reminders:**
- 7 days before an assignment is due (gentle heads up)
- 3 days before (time to start planning)
- 1 day before (time to work on it)
- 6 hours before (finish up!)
- 2 hours before (almost deadline!)

**Where you get reminders:**
- In Chrome (little popup in corner)
- In Discord (if you want)
- Dashboard you can click to see everything

---

## 📖 Step-by-Step Setup (Takes 10 minutes)

### Step 1: Install the Extension (5 minutes)

1. **Open Chrome browser** on your computer
2. **Type this in the address bar:** `chrome://extensions/`
3. **Turn on "Developer mode"** (switch in top-right corner)
4. **Click "Load unpacked"** (blue button on left)
5. **Find this folder and click it:**
   - Look for: `rangekeeper` → `extension`
   - Full path: `/home/ubuntu/.openclaw/workspace/rangekeeper/extension/`
6. **Click "Select"**

You should now see **RangeKeeper** in your extensions list!

**Pin it to your toolbar:**
- Click the puzzle piece icon (🧩) in Chrome
- Find RangeKeeper
- Click the pin 📌

---

### Step 2: Use It (2 minutes)

1. **Go to Blackboard:** `https://ualearn.blackboard.com`
2. **Log in** with your UA username and password
3. **Click around:**
   - Go to your courses
   - Look at assignments
   - Check your calendar
   - Look at grades

**RangeKeeper watches while you browse** — it remembers everything for you!

---

### Step 3: Check Your Dashboard (1 minute)

1. **Click the RangeKeeper icon** in your Chrome toolbar (you pinned it in Step 1)
2. **You'll see:**
   - How many courses you have
   - How many assignments are coming up
   - Which ones are due soon
   - When each assignment is due

**Check this whenever you want** — it's always up to date!

---

### Step 4: Get Notifications (Automatic!)

**You don't need to do anything for this part.**

When an assignment is coming up soon, you'll see:
- A little popup in the corner of Chrome
- It will say: **"Assignment Due Soon: [Name of Assignment] is due in X hours"**

**Click the notification** to go straight to your dashboard and see all your upcoming work.

---

## 🎨 Understanding the Colors

The dashboard uses colors to help you see what's urgent:

- **🟢 Green:** Assignment is far away (more than 3 days)
- **🟣 Purple:** Assignment is coming up (1-3 days)
- **🟠 Orange:** Assignment is due soon (less than 1 day)
- **🔴 Red:** Assignment is due very soon or overdue (less than 6 hours)

## ❓ What If...?

### "I don't see any assignments"

**Possible reasons:**
1. You haven't logged into Blackboard yet → Go to Blackboard and click around
2. Your professors haven't posted assignments yet → This is normal at the start of the semester
3. The extension is having trouble → Open DevTools (F12 key) and look for `[RangeKeeper]` messages

**Ask Dad for help if:**
- You see error messages in red
- The dashboard stays empty after browsing Blackboard for 5 minutes
- The extension icon isn't in your toolbar

---

### "I'm getting too many notifications"

**You can turn them off:**
1. Right-click the RangeKeeper icon
2. Click "Options" or "Settings"
3. Change notification settings

**Or ask Dad** — he can adjust how often you get reminders.

---

### "I want to see my grades too"

**This is coming soon!** Dad is adding grade tracking next.

For now, you'll need to check grades on Blackboard the normal way.

---

### "What if I already finished an assignment?"

**Good question!** Right now, RangeKeeper doesn't know when you submit something.

**You'll still get reminders** until the due date passes.

**Dad is working on** making it stop reminding you once you submit.
